ShapeSaber - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Summary: ShapeSaber does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The only third-party service used is Google AdMob for displaying advertisements.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ShapeSaber ("the Game", "we", "us") handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

ShapeSaber does not require:

  • Account creation or login
  • Name, email, or any personal details
  • Location data
  • Contact information

All game progress (scores, level unlocks, settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

ShapeSaber uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain data as described in their privacy policy:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for approximate location to serve relevant ads)
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob Policies.

4. Children's Privacy

ShapeSaber is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads shown are managed by Google AdMob according to their content policies.

5. Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Settings and preferences
  • Theme selection

This data is never uploaded, shared, or transmitted. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

6. Permissions

ShapeSaber requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET – Required to load and display advertisements
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – Required to check network availability for ads

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
